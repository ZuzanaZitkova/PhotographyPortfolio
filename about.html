About me

Zuzana Zitkova

My photography journey is an unexpected yet beautiful path that unfolded as I embarked on life's adventures. Hailing from the Czech Republic, I pursued a degree in mathematics teaching at university. At the age of 23, I married an amazing man, and together, we took a leap of faith, moving to the stunning landscapes of New Zealand.

It was in this picturesque country, four years ago, that I discovered my passion for photography. Nature's grandeur beckoned me, and I began capturing its essence through my lens. The transition to photographing people and creating simple portraits followed naturally. Each click of the shutter allowed me to tell unique stories.

Now, as I contemplate venturing into product photography, I reflect on this incredible journey. It has been a gradual, organic process that has enriched my life with creativity and joy. Every step has deepened my love for photography, making it an integral part of who I am.
